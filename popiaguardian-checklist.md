## 🏢 PHASE 2: Company Registration & Structure

### Choose Jurisdiction
- [ ] **Decision:** South Africa vs UAE (Dubai) vs Both
  - SA: Local credibility, easier POPIA positioning
  - UAE: Tax advantages, already based there
  - Both: Holding company structure (advanced)

### South Africa Route (Recommended for POPIA credibility)
- [ ] Register **PTY LTD** with CIPC
  - Name reservation (~R50)
  - Company registration (~R175)
- [ ] Get **tax number** from SARS
- [ ] Register for **VAT** (if revenue > R1M/year)
- [ ] Open **business bank account** (FNB, Standard Bank, etc.)
- [ ] Get **B-BBEE certificate** (for government/corporate contracts)

### UAE Route (If optimizing for tax)
- [ ] Choose free zone: IFZA, DMCC, or RAK
- [ ] Trade license application (~AED 5,000-15,000)
- [ ] UAE residence visa (if not already)
- [ ] Corporate bank account (WIO, Liv., Mashreq Neo)

### Structure Decision Matrix
| Factor | SA Only | UAE Only | Dual Structure |
|--------|---------|----------|----------------|
| POPIA Credibility | ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Tax Rate | 27% | 0% | Optimized |
| Cost to Run | Low | Medium | Higher |
| Local Trust | High | Lower | Medium |
| Complexity | Simple | Simple | Complex |

**My Recommendation:** Start with SA PTY LTD for market credibility. UAE can be a holding company later if tax optimization matters.
